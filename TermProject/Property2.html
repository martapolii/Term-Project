Serene Sulani Oasis

201 Island Ave, Sulani

Escape to a world of serenity and seclusion with Serene Sulani Oasis, nestled on the tranquil shores of Sulani. Accessible only by boat, this island sanctuary beckons with its open-concept design, inviting the gentle ocean breeze to dance through its spacious interiors. Step outside onto expansive patios and breathe in the intoxicating scent of tropical blooms, as the azure waves kiss the shores of your private haven. Behold the breathtaking beauty of the coral reef just a stone's throw away, offering an underwater paradise for the adventurous soul. With vistas of both the endless ocean expanse and the majestic volcanic landscape of Sulani, surrender to the allure of island living at its finest.

  • Lot size: 2000 sq. ft.
  • Rooms: 2 bed, 2 bath
  • Ammenities/Features:
    • Private dock
    • 2 floors
    • 2 spacious decks
    • Open concept
    • Sweeping views of the ocean and Sulani
