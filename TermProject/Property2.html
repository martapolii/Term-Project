Serene Sulani Oasis

201 Island Ave, Sulani

Located on the peaceful shores of Sulani, the Serene Sulani Oasis is the perfect place to escape to a world of peace and tranquility. This island sanctuary, which is only reachable by boat, has an airy open-concept layout that lets the soft sea breeze flow through its roomy interiors. Take in the enticing aroma of the ocean as you step outside onto large patios and watch the blue waves.

Admire the stunning nearby coral reef, which provides an unparalleled snorkelling experience. Give in to the charm of island living at its best with views of Sulani's breathtaking volcanic landscape and boundless ocean.

  • Price: $1,500,000
  • Lot size: 2000 sq. ft.
  • Rooms: 2 bed, 2 bath

  • Ammenities/Features:
    • Private dock
    • 2 floors
    • 2 spacious decks
    • Open concept
    • Sweeping views of the ocean and Sulani
    • Private coral reef
